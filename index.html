About Ira Urban Co-operative Credit Society

Niyojit Ira Urban Co-operative Credit Society Limited is a newly established modern cooperative credit institution, founded in July 2025 with the vision of providing secure and transparent financial services to common citizens.

Built on a strong foundation of financial experience and trust, our society has received primary approval and will be fully operational within the next 3 months. We are committed to contributing to the financial progress of our members and the community.

Our approach combines traditional cooperative values with modern banking practices, ensuring that every member receives personalized attention and access to comprehensive financial solutions.

Our Mission

To provide secure and transparent financial services to common citizens and contribute to their financial progress.

Our Vision

To promote financial literacy and work towards the prosperity of our members with complete transparency and trust.
